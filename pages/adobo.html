Chicken Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs) chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 1 head garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 cup water Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Marinate the Chicken:
In a large bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes
2. Cooking the Adobo:
In a pan, saute sliced onions until they translucent. Add the marinated chicken until the chicken is lightly browned.
3. Pour Vinegar and Water:
Add white vinegar and water. Allow it to boil without stirring.
4. Simmer:
Lower the heat, cover the pan, and simmer for about 30 to 40 minutes or until the chicken is tender. Stir occasionally.
5. Seasoning:
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
6. Serve:
Serve the Chicken Adobo with steamed rice.
